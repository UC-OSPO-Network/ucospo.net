## Lightning Intros

Ever wished you could meet everyone at a conference in one go? This is your chance!

During Lightning Intros, each participant gets 30 seconds to introduce themselves to the full group and share what topics they're most excited to talk about. Then, during the reception that follows, you can seek each other out and dive into those conversations.

No slides, no prep—just you and a microphone. A sign-up form will go out via email the week before the event.
